Our Motivation

The main motivation for making this platform is to help people contact skilled labor present in the market. Usually, it is a hassle to find a plumber to get your broken pipes fixed. The reason is that people donâ€™t know who is skilled enough to help them with their utility work. At times they are unsatisfied with the quality of work because they chose an inexperienced person for the job. So, a platform is imperative where any person can post their tasks or find a skilled person from the market by viewing their profile to get their task to be completed. This will help people to choose for themselves who will be doing their job instead of randomly calling out a person without any prior work history. This will abridge a gap between the untapped market and the users who are at the mercy of only a few people they know about.

Target Audience

The stakeholders of this freelance system will primarily be the service providers such as plumbers, electricians, and carpenters. Moreover, the consumers of the services will be directly affected as they are the ones utilizing this software to fulfill their needs. The organization that manages and owns this business will also be the stakeholder as it is their reputation on the line which is why appropriate measures will be taken. As this service is open to be used by everyone, it is going to affect a huge population and has the potential to grow to a nationwide scale.
